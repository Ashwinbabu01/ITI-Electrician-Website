Your Study Roadmap for the ITI Electrician 1st Year CBT Exam

Exam Overview and Format

Exam Composition

The CBT exam primarily focuses on Trade Theory, supplemented by Employability Skills and Workshop Calculation & Science.

  • Approximately 70% of questions are from Trade Theory.
  • Approximately 30% of questions are from Employability Skills and Workshop Calculation & Science.

ITI Electrician 1st Year CBT Format

Section Questions (Approx.) Marks
Trade Theory 35 - 40 70 - 80
Workshop Cal & Science 10 - 15 20 - 30
Employability Skills 10 - 15 20 - 30
TOTAL 60 - 70 MCQs 100 marks

Trade Theory: Most Important Chapters

Scroll horizontally to navigate the Trade Theory roadmap.

1. Basic Electrical

  • Ohm's Law, Voltage, Current, Resistance, Power
  • Focus on Direct formula-based MCQs.

2. Electrical Circuit

  • Series, Parallel Circuits, KVL, KCL
  • Focus on Numericals and Concepts.

3. Magnetism & Electromagnetism

  • Magnetic field, Fleming's rules, Solenoid, EMF
  • Focus on Theory and Principle-based questions.

4. DC Machines

  • DC Motor/Generator - Parts, Working, Application
  • Focus on Matching and Function questions.

5. Electrical Safety

  • Fuse, MCB, Earthing types, Safety signs
  • Focus on Real-life based MCQs.

6. Measuring Instruments

  • Multimeter, Ammeter, Voltmeter, Clamp meter
  • Focus on Identification and Use.

7. Wiring System

  • Wiring types, Tools, Connections
  • Focus on Diagram-based questions.

Workshop Calculation & Science and Employability Skills

Workshop Calculation & Science

Important topics for CBT:

  • Units & Measurements
  • Ohm's Law Numericals
  • Power & Energy: kWh, Watt, Horsepower
  • Mensuration: Area, Volume (Box, Pipe)
  • Trigonometry, Work, Energy, Force
